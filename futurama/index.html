Welcome to My Futurama Fan Site!

Planet Express Crew

Professor Farnsworth

Professor Farnsworth is a senile, deranged, as well as unpredictable old man who is both a maniac and a genius. He has a gift and passion for the creation of Doomsday Devices and Atomic Supermen and is a danger to himself, his employees, as well as the Universe in general.

Philip J. Fry

Fry is generally lazy and spends most of his time in the office sitting on the couch, watching TV, and drinking Slurm. He is "special" and is immune to Brain Slugs because of his lack of Delta Brainwaves.

Turanga Leela

With a "no rain or sleet" attitude and a "kick-your-arse" grace, this delivery captain is a refreshing source of professionalism to counterpoint the rest of the crew.


Dr. Zoidberg

Zoidberg is considered to be the most caring and innocent member of the crew. He is a sweet and lovable guy who just wants a friend to hang out with but for some reason most people dislike him, especially Hermes. Despite this, Zoidberg considers everyone to be his friend.

Bender

Bender is a selfish and insensitive robot who usually cares about no one except himself. He has been known for getting the Planet Express Crew into a lot of trouble due to his scheming, yet rarely shows any signs of remorse.
